Written Anlysis for Heroes of PyMoli

From the analysis performed, I concluded the following from this dataset. 

1. The market is dominated by males, being 84.03% of the player population. 

2. Genders other than male tend to spend more on average total and per purchase.  
 
3. Game is mostly populated with players aged 20-24

4. Age of players is normally distributed, with the youngest player being 7 and oldest player being 45. The average age range is 20-24.

5. Cheaper items are bringing the average price of a purchased item down. The top spenders are over a dollar more expensive.

6. Players in the age range of 35-39 tend to spend more on average.

My recommendation to the creators of Heroes of Pymoli:

Focus on attracting more non-male players to the game, as they will be more profitable players. 
Stay away from adding cheaper items, and maybe try pushing prices up on new items. 
Try to build a player loyalty to the game, and keep players around for longer. The data shows as players get older they will spend more money on average.
